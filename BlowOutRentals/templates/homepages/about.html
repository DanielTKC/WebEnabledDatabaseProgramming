About Us

Founded in 2018, Blowout Rentals began its story in the basement of a one-story home in the suburbs of Provo, Utah. Due to the increasing popularity of music over the last year, Blowout Rentals has grown tremendously and now has several shops across Utah Valley. We offer a wide selection of instrument rentals, ranging from flutes to saxophones and offer various insurance and protection packages to ensure a positive rental experience.

Our mission: A trumpet in every home!
